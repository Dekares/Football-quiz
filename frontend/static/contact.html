Contact

We improve Careerdle as you play it. Got a question, spotted a bug, or does a player's career look incomplete or wrong? Just reach out — your feedback helps us make the game better.

Email

Email is the fastest way. Write to or copy the address below:

emoaslan23@gmail.com

What to write about

  • Bug reports: something not working right in the game?
  • Data fixes: a player's clubs, position or nationality looks incorrect?
  • Suggestions: a new mode or feature you'd like to see.
  • Collaboration: advertising, content or project proposals.

The more detail you add (a screenshot, which mode, which player), the faster we can help. We usually reply within a few days.
